Iron Bars About

Ironbars.org was created by Arian Flores for the final capstone project to be presented on Hiring Day, March 29, 2016

Ironbars.org uses 2010 US Census data and statistics from the BOJ’s National Prisoner Statistics program to display your probability of being incarcerated based on demographics (i.e gender, race, location). I’ve built this app to bring focus to the mass incarceration burden US prisons face today. My hope is that this app will help shed some light on data not often brought up on any political platform. If you are interested in learning more about this issue visit www.prisonpolicy.org.
