Iron Bars About

Ironbars.org uses data from the 2010 US Census, prisonpolicy.org, and the BOJ’s National Prisoner Statistics program to display your likelyhood of being incarcerated based on demographics (i.e gender, race, location).

I’ve built this app to help shed some light on data not often brought up on any political platform when discussing mass incarceration. If you are interested in learning more about this issue visit www.prisonpolicy.org.
